# Velocity converter

Veclocity converter for Wheel motor

## Pin Assignments

|Pin|GPIO|Function|
|:---:|:---:|:---|
|7 |GPIO 4 |DIGITAL OUT (RESET MOTOR DRIVER)|
|16|GPIO 23|DIGITAL OUT (INITIALIZE CONTROLLER)|
|15|GPIO 22|DIGITAL OUT (SPI CS)|
|19|GPIO 10|SPI0 MOSI|
|21|GPIO 9 |SPI0 MISO|
|23|GPIO 11|SPI0 SCLK|
